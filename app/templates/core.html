What is a CORE Software?

CORE is Employee management software which is used to manage all employees from top level to bottom level. Several modules are associated with this BPM tool, allowing the work force to be divided into layers. This gives the authority to measure the ability of the employees and manage the time flow of work done effectively by transferring duties from one level to another. We offer a platform that is flexible and feature rich for controlling and managing an entire business from anywhere in the world.
